# Cybersecurity Learning Roadmap

**A self-directed cybersecurity curriculum adapted from a local university program and complemented with practice platforms, communities, certification providers, and specialization options.**

## About

I originally created this roadmap to reproduce independently, through self-study and practice, the learning progression of a newly introduced cybersecurity degree at a local university.

This repository reorganizes that curriculum into a public reference. It preserves the original structure of terms, workshops, specialization subjects, certifications, resources, communities, and providers contained in my source spreadsheets.

## Curriculum

### First training cycle

#### Term 1

- English I
- Mathematical Foundations
- Cybersecurity Fundamentals
- Introduction to Operating Systems and Networks
- Operating System Cybersecurity
- Communication and Digital Skills
- Mathematics I

#### Term 2

- English II
- Introduction to Penetration Testing
- Exploits and Post-Exploitation
- Probability and Statistics
- Network Cybersecurity
- Cybersecurity Governance
- Mathematics II

#### Term 3

- English III
- Network Traffic Analysis
- Technology Risk Management
- Identity and Access Control
- Data Protection
- Integrative Project I
- Mathematics III

### Second training cycle

#### Term 4

- English IV
- Social Engineering
- Critical Security Controls I
- Secure Development Foundations
- Hacking Foundations
- DevSecOps
- Intelligence and National Security Foundations
- Mathematics IV

#### Term 5

- English V
- Physical Hacking
- Vulnerability Management
- Cyberdefense Foundations
- Critical Security Controls II
- Open-Source Intelligence Collection Techniques (OSINT)
- Integrative Project II
- Mathematics V

#### Term 6

- Technical University Degree Internship in Network Security
- Mathematics VI

### Third training cycle

#### Term 7

- English VI
- Web Application Hacking
- Cyberdefense Architecture
- Threat Management
- Cryptography
- Compliance

#### Term 8

- English VII
- Mobile Application Hacking

- Incident Response and Management

- English VIII

- Bachelor's Degree Internship in Cybersecurity Engineering
- Mathematics X
